Asian Art Museum Book Store

The Asian Art Museum Store has over 1,000 titles in stock. One of the most extensive collections of books covering Asian art, culture, religion, craft, food and literature. The online store highlights an overview of what is in store. In addition to our in store collection, we've added a Bookshop.org page. By including a Bookshop.org affiliates page in our menu, we can offer titles from 7.5 million more titles. We create book lists based on recommendations from our staff, docents, curators, and members. The additional revenue from our affiliates page supports the Museum. To access this extended list of titles, click here: Bookshop

New Books

Browse the newest fiction & nonfiction kids and adults titles. In addition to our Asia focused offerings, we are part of the Bookshop.org. Our bookshop.org page includes of course book recommendations from our staff, docents and curators. In addition, our Bookshop.org page enables you to order over 5 million general interest titles. Books are mailed direct to you from our partner warehouses. (Bookshop.org does not allow for in-store pickup yet, but we hope to in the not too distant future) Bookshop.org's interface and purchase process is designed to be as convenient, streamlined, and user-friendly as possible. Each time you make a purchase on Bookshop.org, a portion of your purchase goes to support the Asian Art Museum.
