GALLERY

In the Galleries

The museum's collection galleries on the second and third floors feature more than 2,000 artworks from all the major cultures of Asia. Artworks in the galleries are regularly rotated, offering visitors a chance to view more of the museum's collection.

Masterpieces

What constitutes a “masterpiece” is a personal judgment. We've chosen some works to bring to your attention because they are beautiful objects that we have found to be rewarding to look at over many years, important historically, or rare—you won't often have the chance to see works of their type and quality.
